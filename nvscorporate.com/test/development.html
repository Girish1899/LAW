  STRATEGIC HUMAN RESOURCES:

At NVS MACS, we strongly believe in developing talent within organizations. We believe that effectively optimizing and managing talent is the key to sustainable development for all organizations and customize each of our services after carefully understanding the specific talent requirements of every one of our clients while offering them the following services:

Training and Development: We closely coordinate with our clients to identify the training objectives of their organization and design and develop workshops to address these needs. We follow up on the progress of our trainees, analyze their application of the newly learnt skills on the job and assist our clients in integrating the results into their performance appraisal systems. Our specialized trainers provide training content for domestic and international clients who want to conduct their training programs internally.

Knowledge Management Initiatives: We ensure that organizations are self-sufficient and face sustainable development in the dynamic business environment. Our consultants are highly professed in assisting clients with global workforce and mobility management, especially for international and multinational clients with a wide geographical presence. We implement knowledge management initiatives such as succession planning and management, mentoring and coaching to ensure that our clients are able to develop their internal competencies to be able to competitively position themselves.

Competency Profiling: We identify job and organizational competencies to ensure that all the activities of the organization are performed by people with relevant skills and help enforce organizational values on all employees. Job-specific competencies are integrated into their job profiles and descriptions, which are developed based on the context. On the other hand, organizational competencies will provide parameters during our clients’ recruitment process to ensure that the capabilities of all employees are aligned with the strategy of their organizations.

Recruitment and Selection: Our recruiters assist organizations with their recruitment process by soliciting candidates, conducting interviews and narrowing down on the most suitable profile for the job at hand, for our clients to choose from. The interview process can be an iterative one with multiple levels of tests and interview questions, carefully structured by our consultants with our clients.



STRATEGIC BUSINESS PLANNING:

Organizations need to develop the right business strategy to successfully traverse the stages of their business life cycle. NVS MACS assist companies to take strategic decisions that will provide them sustained growth by means of the following services:

Outsourcing Initiatives: NVS MACS assists clients in segregating their activities into core and non-core operations. We help our clients understand their non-core operations with relevance to outsourcing by analyzing whether the option would be better than performing functions in-house, identifying the activities that could be outsourced, discussing the nature of outsourcing and other essential aspects of business processes. We help our clients identify suitable partners with whom they can build tactical or strategic relationships, based on their outsourcing initiatives.

Global Partners and Opportunities: We assist our domestic clients to develop an international presence by identifying the right global business partners and opportunities and also assist our international clients develop their presence in India through partnerships and foreign investments. We guide and assist clients in drafting proposals and conducting due diligence, depending on the nature of newly developed business relationships with other entities.

 Finance and Tax Advice: Our consultants assist clients with their investment structuring at different stages of their business and develop realistic financial plans that will help them plan for contingencies. We also offer guidance and advice on onshore and offshore taxes, including inter-state tax regulations, and assist clients in locating special economic zones that are appropriate to their business.



INSURANCE AND RISK MANAGEMENT:

Every organization needs to ensure that it minimizes its risk to the maximum possible extent because of the dynamics and uncertainty of the business environment. It can sometimes become tedious for our clients to set up an insurance department just for the purpose of providing ad hoc advice and support. To mitigate these problems, NVS MACS assists clients with their insurance requirements. Organizations seek advice and guidance from our insurance and legal consultants in the following areas:

Legal Representation: We assist clients by representing them in any dispute as ‘Claim Consultants’ and liaise with insurers/loss adjusters to ensure that their claims are appropriately addressed. Our legal consultants also facilitate settlement of cases out of court by liaising with claimants and providing advice on policy liability.

Legal Liabilities: We provide dedicated advice to our clients on legal liabilities under various Acts and suggest appropriate risk management measures addressing areas, such as Insurance Law, Labour Law, Law of Carriage of Goods and Environment Protection Act, to state a few.

Registration Formalities: Our consultants assist insurance companies, brokers and other parties in their license registration formalities.

Advice on Benefits: We guide and advice our clients depending on their specific requirements in the areas of property, liability, marine and employee benefits in terms of:

   •  Program Design & Placement
   •  Risk Review & Identification
   •  Analysis & Recommendation

Project Insurance Management: We provide specific advice during all phases of Project Insurance Management for our clients and assist them with accounting and stock verification for Surveyors and Loss Adjusters.

Risk Assessment: We help clients in identifying and measuring their total cost of risk to facilitate them in their planning process. Our consultants carefully inspect existing situations with clients and provide advice on:

   •  Project Consultancy
   •  Document Review
   •  Lender(s) Requirements (Negotiations)
   •  Contract Review / Wordings
   •  Recommended Program Review
   •  Operational Insurance Integration
   •  Risk Management Procedures



LEGAL SUPPORT:

With the able support of our sister concern, NVS & Associates, we negotiate and provide advice on all types of commercial transactions in our clients’ businesses. Corporates seek our advice and guidance to resolve legal issues in these areas:

Corporate and Securities Laws: We advise our clients on the enforceability of inter-company guarantees and security, draft covenants involving international trade and commerce and act for and on behalf of the companies, advising on powers, duties and liabilities of Directors.

Litigation: We appear and argue on behalf of our clients before appropriate authorities and assist them in any litigation concerning banking and insurance, international or commercial disputes, property-related disputes, intellectual property rights (IPR) and employment laws.

Banking-Related Issues: We provide advice for appropriate action on all banking-related issues and are involved in the preparation and approval of loan documents and assessment of titles of collateral securities. We also assist our clients in complying with the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) and handle and advice on all appeals under the SARFEASI Act.

Cyber Law and Information Security: Apart from providing advice and guidance in matters related to preparation of electronic contracts and cyber crime protection, we also help our clients structure electronic contracts, prepare contracts pertaining to hardware sale, purchase, rental, lease, installation, testing, maintenance, distribution and marketing, draft contracts pertaining to website design, development and hosting and prepare Network Service Level Agreements.

Property Laws: We advise, assist and prepare property acquisition and disposal documents, assist in commercial leasing and help our clients with other property-related legal issues.

Environment Laws: We advise our clients on laws, rules, regulations and governmental incentives available to further environmental interests, including tax credits, grants and land use provisions.

Employment Laws: We assist our clients in handling various employment-related legal issues such as drafting employment contracts, retiral benefits, obtaining work permits and permission for immigration, employee disputes, dismissal, discharge and termination of service contracts and employee confidentiality agreements.

Intellectual Property Rights Protection: We provide advice on registration for IPR, covenant drafting for commercial exploitation and licensing and enforcement of IPR.

Taxation Laws: We provide advice and guidance with regard to central excise and customs, such as the levy and collection of duty under appropriate conditions and with regard to direct taxes in assessing assets and advising on payments.

Bankruptcy and Corporate Restructuring: We advise our clients on winding up proceedings and legal validity of all measures, as well as all matters before Board of Industrial & Financial Reconstruction (BIFR), Appellate Industrial & Financial Reconstruction (AIFR) and High Courts.

Legal Auditing: We analyze corporate compliance, scrutinize, examine and verify the documents, their interpretation and reporting from our clients’ point of view.

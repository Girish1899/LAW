NVS MACS is a leading consulting firm that offers its services to domestic and international companies that want to establish or expand business in India. We assist companies, right from incorporation to strategic business development, including talent management and development, offering customised services at all stages of business.

NVS MACS was established by N. V. Srinivasan to help companies run their businesses smoothly by concentrating on their core activities rather than investing time on the cumbersome peripheral processes of business establishment and development. We have developed a strong culture of trust with our clients and believe in forging long-term relationships with all our stakeholders. At NVS MACS, we believe in developing dedicated partnerships with our clients by offering highly customised and timely solutions to help them run their operations smoothly.

Everyone at NVS MACS is a competent leader with strong analytical and decision-making capabilities and works efficiently to deliver effective and high quality business solutions. We strive to offer our clients a fulfilling experience by providing a wide range of services as separate products or as a collaborated effort, based on each client’s requirements.
At NVS MACS, we help you realise your vision.

  • Manage your talent for better business growth and development
  • Develop an integrated value system to address your business needs
  • Offer specific solutions to provide a fulfilling business experience
  • Provide insurance and risk management advice at all stages of business

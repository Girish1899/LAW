If you aspire to work with a dedicated, committed and competent group of people, then NVS MACS is the place for you. Our growth is directly aligned to the growth and development of our people as they are our strongest assets.

At NVS MACS we believe in variety learning and the functional diversity of our firm allows us to develop a multitude of skills and knowledge throughout our careers and provides ample opportunities for further growth. We encourage innovation and creativity and nurture and develop intrapreneurs within the firm. Every member is empowered to perform activities in the best way possible to develop efficient systems to satisfy client needs, while working within the value system of the firm and receiving constant support through mentoring and coaching.

INTERNSHIP

At NVS MACS, we offer internship opportunities for commerce / business students to develop their skills and prepare them for the corporate environment. The people at NVS MACS act as mentors and coaches to new interns, establishing a mutually beneficial relationship. These internship programs are ideally between 4 to 12 weeks, taking into consideration the academic calendars of the students.

If you are interested in applying for an internship, please send your resume to: jobs@nvscorporate.com.
